Taj Mahal

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Agra that is a symbol of the love between Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan and Queen Mumtaz

Red Fort

A colossal monument in Delhi that showcases the supremacy of Mughal craftsmanship

Qutub Minar

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi that is one of the tallest towers in the world

Humayun's Tomb

A tomb in Delhi that features elaborate corridors, kiosks, domes, and fine detailings

Hawa Mahal

A five-story structure in Jaipur that blends beauty and Rajasthan culture

Sanchi Stupa

A huge monument in Buddhist history in Madhya Pradesh that is famed for its grand hemispherical dome, ornate gateways, and carvings


Jallianwala Bagh

A historic site in Amritsar, Punjab that commemorates the massacre of hundreds of innocent civilians by British forces on April 13, 1919

Chittorgarh Fort

One of the biggest forts in India in Rajasthan that has within this fort complex a number of palaces, temples, and towers

Bhimbetka

Believed to be the oldest historical place in India, located in Madhya Pradesh, with rock shelters dating back to the Paleolithic age
